## Summary

Duarte et al. analyze fixed-income arbitrage strategies including swap
spread trades. The swap spread is the difference between the swap rate
and the Treasury yield at the same maturity.

## Phase 1 proxy

Uses trailing return cross-sectional ranking as carry proxy. True
implementation needs swap and Treasury rate data. See ADR-001.
