# Known failure modes — swap_spread_carry

## 1. Rate regime sensitivity
The swap spread can become persistently negative (as in 2015-2016).

## 2. Proxy limitation
Trailing return doesn't capture actual swap-Treasury spread.

## 3. Counterparty risk
Swap trades involve counterparty exposure.

## 4. Low-frequency signal
Monthly rebalance may miss intra-month spread movements.
