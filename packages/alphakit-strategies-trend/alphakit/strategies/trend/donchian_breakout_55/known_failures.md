# Known failure modes — donchian_breakout_55

## 1. Long drawdowns between trends

The 55-day window is long. During the 2015–2016 commodity range,
Turtle System 2 stayed flat on the commodity complex for months
while the 20-day variant was whipsawing — but when the range finally
broke, System 2 captured it cleanly. The flip side: expect 12-18
month stretches of zero activity in quiet markets.

## 2. Late into reversals

The 20-day exit is still slow compared to actively managed stops.
A 2022-style rate shock that reverses a bond trend in 3 weeks will
punish the strategy for ~2 weeks before the exit signal fires.

## 3. Data-snooping warning (inherited from all Donchian variants)

Curtis Faith's book is descriptive of a specific program that used
these parameters. Post-publication out-of-sample performance of
literal Turtle rules has been weaker than the 1983–1988 trader-program
returns Faith reports.
